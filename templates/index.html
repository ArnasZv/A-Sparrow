CAR MEETS IRELAND

Car Meets Ireland is a responsive event-based website I designed and built for car enthusiasts to connect and participate in automotive meetups. The site allows users to register their interest, submit details about their vehicles, and get involved in upcoming events. I focused on creating a clean, engaging UI with smooth user flows for event signups and car submissions. I also integrated an e-commerce section where users can browse and purchase custom event merchandise, such as shirts, decals, and accessories. This project highlights my skills in front-end development, user experience design, and building dynamic, community-driven platforms.

GAMING IRELAND

Gaming Ireland is a vibrant, content-rich website I designed and developed to promote one of Ireland’s largest gaming conventions. The site serves as a central hub where users can learn about the event, view the schedule, book tickets, and explore featured guests, streamers, and tournaments. I focused on delivering an engaging, immersive experience through bold visuals, dynamic content sections, and responsive design optimized for both desktop and mobile. I also implemented a merchandise store for exclusive Gamer Fest apparel and collectibles, as well as seamless ticket integration and real-time updates for event announcements. This project showcases my ability to design and build interactive event platforms that cater to large, diverse audiences in the gaming community.

IRON KINGDOM GYM

Iron Kingdom Gym is a modern, high-impact website I designed and developed for a locally-owned gym based in Longford, Ireland. The goal was to create a bold, motivational online presence that reflects the gym’s no-nonsense training ethos and strong community culture. The site features membership sign-up options, class schedules, trainer bios, and an integrated booking system for personal training sessions. I designed the layout with a clean, muscular aesthetic and optimized it for fast performance and mobile responsiveness. Additionally, I built out an online store for branded gym gear, including hoodies, supplements, and accessories. This project demonstrates my ability to deliver high-performance websites tailored to local businesses with strong branding and user-focused functionality
